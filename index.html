Campus Life

School Church

Established in 1888

Marist Catholic College North Shore is committed to empowering students to make a difference in their world through Social Justice initiatives across K–12. We are guided by St Mary of the Cross MacKillop’s directive to “never see a need without doing something about it.” We live this out through engaging with a range of Catholic ministries that include: St Vincent de Paul, Jesuit Social Services, Caritas and Marist Solidarity.



school library

our academic resource centre

Our Academic Resource Centre provides a social space to meet, collaborate, research, learn, share, and relax. The staff work to develop a reading culture and raise the levels of information literacy across the school by leading students through processes which empower them to be critical thinkers, enthusiastic readers and skillful, efficient and discerning researchers.



school hall

made for important events

This place stores many important memories of our school whether it comes to leadership announcements, start or end of the year masses, and so on.

Mercer Laboratories Tech Girls Competition
Won the National Primary School Division in the Tech Girls Competition

This year one of our Year 5 teams, Just Girls (TG5-2) won the National Primary School Division with their app design and business plan! Well done Annabel Vu, Abigail Blythe, Victoria Cheng, Catelyn Anggadi and Kaia Broad. They were mentored by Chiara Longhi, Amazon Web Services; who attended the showcase and heard the announcement live as well. An amazing achievement.

Hypatia Challenge at Marist Sisters’ College Woolwich
awarded overall winners in both Year 4 and Year 5

An exciting academic gala day inspired by Hypatia of Alexandria - a pioneering woman in the fields of Mathematics and Astronomy and symbol of intellectual curiosity and innovation. We are thrilled and honoured to announce that both teams were victorious and awarded overall winners in both Year 4 and Year 5. Winning in both categories is testament to the girls commitment to working as a team and pushing the boundaries of knowledge and creativity, just as Hypatia did. Congratulations to both teams on such a magnificent achievement!
